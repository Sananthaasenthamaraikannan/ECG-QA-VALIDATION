Both detectors were matched to expert beats within a 50 ms window. The baseline uses a single threshold set from the whole-record maximum; it misses beats where amplitude dips over the 30-minute recording. The moving-window method thresholds against a local 2-second maximum, recovering nearly all beats. Note: the window parameters were tuned on this record and not validated on unseen records — production ECG labs pair automated detection with human over-read.
